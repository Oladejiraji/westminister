What services do you provide?

We guide students bound for secondary school, college, and graduate school. We also work with students with special needs, whether it is a learning difference or one requiring a therapeutic placement. Additionally, we work with students seeking to transfer from one college to another.
We offer a range of services from a single session consultation to our comprehensive guidance service, will take the student through the entire admissions process. Depending on individual needs, GESA consults will provide guidance on a student’s academic program, an appropriate list of schools or colleges, a standardized test schedule, advice on extra-curricular and summer activities, essay guidance and interview preparation.

What makes you different from other consultants?

GESA consults uniquely focuses both on college admissions and scholarship advising. We’re backed by over a years of experience, hundreds of satisfied clients, and we proudly stand behind our. We want you to be satisfied - there are never any long-term commitments to work with us, and if you have any concerns, we will make things right.
In some ways that's a tough question because we can't and don't analyze how other consultants conduct their business. Overall clients and professionals comment about how passionate we are about educational consulting and about our commitment.

How do you work with clients?

We are available seven days a week, 52 weeks a year and respond promptly to phone calls, emails and faxes. We have worked with students from across Europe North America, Asia, and South America. If you have a telephone or a computer with a connection to the Internet, we can work with you.

What about confidentiality?

We never identify our clients to anyone, under any circumstances. All communications between GESA consults and our clients are kept strictly confidential. The only time we reveal a client is if a client chooses to recommend us, and even then only if the client explicitly grants us permission to publish his/her name.

Are you one of those "scholarship search" operations that charges money to find scholarships?

No. GESA consults provides advice to students about how they can find scholarships on their own. You receive the search tools you need to locate scholarships all through high school, college and graduate school. Our philosophy is that a student should be able to use the skills we teach them in order to locate suitable scholarship opportunities.

Can GESA consults guarantee admission?

No one can guarantee admission. IECA consultants work with students, and when appropriate, admissions staff, to find the most appropriate schools for that individual- the best fit academically, socially, and financially.
