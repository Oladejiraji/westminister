A few words about us

Global Employment and Scholarship Agency (GESA) is a consultancy service company geared towards the provision of research, writing and logistics consultancy for students and intending workers who seek for greener pastures around the world. the global education and employment climate requires candidate with well- articulated motivations applicable and in-demand skills as well as the curated and eye-catching curriculum vitae which GESA helps to develop in its clientele.

Our Services

Professional Development

With a hyper competitive global job market, candidates need all the help they can get. At GESA we aid the employment prospects of candidates in a variety of ways. We provide training for interviews, develop the public image -Twitter, LinkedIn etc, and improve marketability of resumes & CVs. Tailor-made product offerings which suit the peculiar needs of our clients are always available upon consultation to get that job anywhere in the world.

Educational Consulting

Access admissions into the best countries for your career advancement today at ease. We scrutinize the qualifications for candidates and guarantee admissions to great schools around the world. Tired of the numerous forms, language barriers or tedious bureaucracies slowing down your admission? Contact us and succeed hassle free.
