A few words about us

Global Employment and Scholarship Agency (GESA) is a consultancy service company geared towards the provision of research, writing and logistics consultancy for students and intending workers who seek for greener pastures around the world. the global education and employment climate requires candidate with well- articulated motivations applicable and in-demand skills as well as the curated and eye-catching curriculum vitae which GESA helps to develop in its clientele.

Meet Our Team

Adedoyin

Adedoyin holds a Bachelors Degree in Political Science and International Relations from Osun state University. He has a Post Graduate Diploma in Customer Relationship Management from the Chartered Institute of Customer Relationship Management. Originally starting his career with the Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry as a Trade Promotion Consultant, he currently doubles as a Board Member of the company and the Business Development Manager of GESA.

Adekola

Adekola holds a first-class Bachelors Degree in Political Science and International Relations from Osun State University, Osogbo, Nigeria. He was awarded the Helmut-Schmidt Public Policy and Good Governance Scholarship for his Masters in Democratic Governance and Civil Society at the University of Osnabrueck, Lower Saxony, Germany. He currently pursues a Masters degree in Business Administration at the University of the People in California, United States of America. He doubles as a Board Member of the company and a Senior Consultant in the European and North American Market for GESA.

David

David holds a Bachelors Degree in Microbiology from the University of Calabar, Masters in Medical and Veterinary Microbiology from the University of Ilorin and is enrolled for a Masters in Biomedical Science from the University of Chester United Kingdom. David is a Senior Consultant at GESA.
